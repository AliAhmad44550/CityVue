CityVue Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 07/30/2023

At CityVue, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data when you use our video sharing app. By using CityVue, you agree to the terms and practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using our app.

1. Information We Collect:

a. Account Information: When you sign up for a CityVue account, we collect your email address, username, and password.

b. Profile Information: You have the option to provide additional information to customize your profile, such as a profile picture, bio, and links to social media accounts.

c. Video Content: We collect the videos you upload and share on CityVue, along with any associated metadata (e.g., location, date, and time of upload).

d. Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you use CityVue, including the features you interact with, the content you view, and your interactions with other users.

e. Device Information: We gather device-specific information, including your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

f. Log Data: Our servers automatically record certain log information, such as your IP address, browser type, access times, and referring URLs.

2. How We Use Your Information:

a. Providing Services: We use the information we collect to deliver and improve our services, personalize your experience, and enhance CityVue's features.

b. Communication: We may use your email address to send you important updates, notifications, and promotional materials related to CityVue. You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

c. Content Sharing and Engagement: Your videos and profile information will be visible to other CityVue users, in accordance with your privacy settings. Interactions with other users, such as likes, comments, and messages, are enabled through the information you provide.

d. Analytics: We use analytics to better understand how users engage with CityVue, allowing us to improve and optimize our app's performance and features.

e. Legal Compliance: We may use and disclose your information as required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure:

a. With Other Users: Your profile information, videos, and interactions with other users will be shared with other CityVue users based on your privacy settings.

b. Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us deliver and improve CityVue's services. These providers are contractually obligated to keep your information secure and confidential.

c. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

d. Legal Compliance: We may share your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal proceedings.

4. Data Security:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy:

CityVue is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us, and we will promptly remove the information.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes via email or app notification. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

7. Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or CityVue's privacy practices, please contact us at cityvue@hotmail.com.

By using CityVue, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy, and you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein.
